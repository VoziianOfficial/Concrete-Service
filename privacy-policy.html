How SlabWay handles submitted request information.

This policy applies to information collected through this website, including the contact/request form and standard website technologies. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

Company: SlabWay Matching Group LLC ID: SWM-482710

1. Overview

SlabWay operates as an independent concrete provider-matching platform. The website allows visitors to submit project details so those details may be used to help connect them with participating independent local providers.

SlabWay does not provide concrete installation, repair, inspection, construction, engineering, contractor, or warranty services. Any provider relationship is between the homeowner and the independent provider.

2. Information We May Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily submit through the website, as well as basic technical information that helps operate and protect the site.

  • Name, email address, phone number, and submitted project message.
  • Selected concrete category, such as driveways, patios, slabs, repair, stamped concrete, or walkways.
  • General project details, including approximate scope, location notes, timing preferences, and surface concerns.
  • Consent confirmation submitted through the contact/request form.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, timestamps, and page interactions.
Contact details Used to process the submitted request and communicate about provider-matching options.
