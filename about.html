Platform statement

SlabWay helps homeowners begin concrete requests without direct-service confusion.

We are not a concrete installer, repair company, inspection company, construction crew, or direct contractor. SlabWay is a request and provider-matching platform.

Homeowners can use the site to describe a driveway, patio, slab, repair, stamped concrete, or walkway request. Participating providers are independent and provide their own pricing, scheduling, warranties, availability, and service terms.

Independent, clear, and comparison-focused.

SlabWay is designed around homeowner choice. Submitting a request does not create a service agreement, and homeowners should verify provider license, insurance, qualifications, and written terms before hiring.

Why it exists

Concrete projects often need more context than a quick category label.

A driveway request can involve removal, slope, drainage, access, finish, and timing. A patio request can involve backyard entry, surface connection, preparation, and decorative goals. SlabWay helps homeowners organize these details before comparing provider options.

  • Request-first flow.
    Start with useful project details instead of vague direct-service claims.
  • Comparison-friendly structure.
    Review provider-supplied scope, timing, availability, and terms before deciding.
  • Homeowner control.
    You choose whether to continue, ask more questions, compare further, or stop.
